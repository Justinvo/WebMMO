Welcome to WebMMO's privacy policy. It's going to be a pretty short one as we basically save no data on our users, except for anonymous game data.

What do we store:

When you sign in with Google a anonymous user-id is created (like this: kkvCbkfCevf6AF4GRnMkmkHj2E04). This user-id is used to store your game-data on our server.

The only other things we store on our server are data related to your gameplay. Things like your health, XP, level, items, quest progress.

WebMMO does not delete user accounts automatically to prevent you from losing progress. If you would like your account to be deleted please reach out at vanoort.justin on gmail.com

What do we track:

WebMMO uses Google Analytics to track where our users are playing from (only on a country level, not a closer location) and how long they spent in the game.
